Comfortable Living in a Caring Community

Minnesota Terrace Apartments offers a peaceful, friendly community of Washington, D.C. residents committed to fair rules, quiet enjoyment, and the natural beauty of the Fort Dupont Park area. Our garden-style walk-up apartments feature secure doors, landscaped grounds, fenced perimeters, and natural privacy barriers.

Why Live Here?

  • 🌳 Short walk or bike ride to Fort Dupont Park
  • 🚇 MetroBus & MetroRail routes nearby for easy travel
  • 🅿️ Plenty of off-street private parking
  • 🛍️ Close to shopping, entertainment, sports centers, and medical facilities
  • 🏡 Secure, well-maintained garden-style buildings
